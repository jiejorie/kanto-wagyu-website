FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

WHAT ARE YOUR HOURS OF OPERATION?

SUNDAY - SATURDAY: 11:00 AM - 11:00 PM

DO YOU ACCEPT RESERVATIONS?

Yes, you can book a reservation by visiting the homepage and click the 'Book Now' button. It will direct you to a form where you can reserve a table for any occasion.

DO YOU OFFER DELIVERY OR TAKEAWAY?

Yes, you can order for delivery by visiting the menu page, scrolling down until you see a delivery section and click the 'Order Now' button. It will direct you to a list of our delivery partners to bring you food straight to your door.

DO YOU OFFER VEGETARIAN OR VEGAN OPTIONS?

We offer a selection of vegetarian-friendly dishes. However, these options are limited; please ask our team for guidance.

DO YOU ACCOMMODATE FOOD ALLERGIES?

Yes, please inform our team of any dietary restrictions when ordering, and we will do our best to accommodate your needs. While we take care in preparation, we cannot guarantee a completely allergen-free environment.

WHAT MAKES OUR WAGYU SPECIAL?

Our wagyu is carefully selected for its quality and balance, offering exceptional flavor with thoughtful value.

IS PARKING AVAILABLE NEARBY?

Yes, there is available parking in front and around the restaurant.

DO YOU OFFER OUTDOOR SEATING?

Yes, we offer outdoor seating in a terrace prompted outside the restaurant. We recommend dining in the terrace during winter.
